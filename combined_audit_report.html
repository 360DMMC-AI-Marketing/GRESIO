Final Verdict

FreshStockAI has a solid architectural foundation with clean REST API design, thoughtful data models, and robust Firebase authentication. However, two critical 500 errors (compliance audit score and inventory seed) and a completely missing AI Pricing backend prevent the product from being market-ready. Core features that do work (settings, vendors, storage locations, margin alerts) are well-implemented and production-ready. With focused effort, FreshStockAI could become a compelling solution for small to medium restaurants within 1–2 weeks.
